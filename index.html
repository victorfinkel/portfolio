PROCESS

UX Design is about building user-friendly products and services taking into account the client's specifications. It is a discipline mixing design, technology and business always prioritizing the User. You will find below a preview of the UX Design process and some implementations in the "Work" section.

1. Understand

Understand client's objectives, benchmark competitors, become acquainted with technical possibilities.

Learn from your users expectations with interviews, survey, statistics, personas, experiences map.

2. Define

Brainstorm with the team on the concept. How you want to help your user's life. Define goal's solution.

Include marketing and developement teams to have a wide angle on the product/service and avoid issues during production's time.

3. Diverge

Organize Design Sprint (paper is a friend there), brainstorm on the interface. Understand how people see the interface, what they would like to see, how they will interact with it.

Iterate this design part.

4. Decide

Apply Users's researches (personas, experience map) to interface, take in consideration content, interactivity, animation.

Decide information architecture, users path.

5. Prototype

Build the wireframes low definition. Understand what can be improve with constructive feedbacks.

Organize the KPI, do some AB/Testing. Make corrections to improve the service or product.

6. Validate

Validate the concept and the interface. Build the graphical layer and launch the product.

Analyze after release to see how it performs.
