Tesla Powerwall

Study case

Create a 360° communication

#solar-energy #electricity #worldchange

Tesla Brand would like to create an online 360° communication, innovative and creative, to launch the Power Wall. The request is to create an innovative webapp to promote its distribution and adoption by french users.

Product : Tesla Powerwall Elon Musk introduce Powerwall and Powerpack

We were asked to :

  • Introduce the product : Powerwall
  • Show some application fields
  • Hightlight the creativity of Tesla
  • Communicate the values of Tesla
  • Promote pre-orders
  • To buzz

About UX Design, the teacher challenged us with a mobile first approach, taking in consideration progressive app strategy and adaptative design.

Process

1. Deepen the brief

  1. Start from the product

    The Power Wall is a new system, which convert sun light to electricity and store it in a domestic battery. This battery feed all the energy-need of the house, even enough powerfull to load the electric car. We decide to focus on this lighting concept.

  2. Make researches

    To propose an disruptive Tesla experience, we dig into this brand to know what makes it unique :

    • How this brand came out ?
    • On which values was it built ?
    • Which final users are aimed ?

  3. Get in Elon Musk's shoes for few days

    Elon Musk is a big contractor, he started with SpaceX, company dealing with spatial researches, Tesla is another huge project to bring electricty for the whole planet in a ecological way. We had to think like a massive powerfull brand.

  4. Build a campaign

    We decided to prototype an adaptative design for a webapp on the concept of : spreading the light accross the world in several stages : Turn on, Spread, Load, Commit.

2. A progressive experience

  • Beginning of the disruptive experience

    Start the compaign with a Tweet on Tesla Account (784k followers should make some noise) about the first stage.

  • Digital experience : Tesla Webapp

    Experience around the webapp, users are receptive to their power to change the world.

  • Convince users of the benefits for the planet

    The user can produce their own energy with much less pollution. They decide to get involved in a futur world change.

  • Commit to a cause

    Spread the word. Promote Pre-order and propose a social experiment with the product.

3. Discover the webapp

  • Step 1 : Spread the light

    Countdown to spread the light around 1 week. Each user can accelerate the process by spreading the light. Earth's globe lights up. If users shares the webapp, he can accelerate again a bit the coundown.

  • Step 2 : Discover and load your Power Wall

    At the end of the countdown, the webapp update to the new step introducing the Power Wall with his specifications. The user can load his own Powerwall. An animation would show him the battery loading, and his position on the earth's globe.

    The idea is that the user share the campaign, so we asked him to share at least one time at this step.

  • Step 3 : Thanks the participants : ambassadors of a world change

    There is a little infographics of the possibilities of the Powerwall which explained all the use we can make of it. It's the end of the campaign so we can show all the light accross the world light up by the participants. We proposed a pre-order access, and a little teaser for the next step which is a physical meeting with the product.

Conclusion

4. Build a long-term relationship

  • Propose a physical event

    To allow the french population to discover the product, we tought about a social experiment. People will be invited to contribute building a solar board by adding a little part.

    Observe the Powerwall is loading faster after and invite them to pass by when they finish work.

    Reward participants with colorfull light animation starting at night time. These animation would be feed by the Powerwall wich stored during the day

  • Propose an online event

    For those who can't come to the physical place, plan an online event to allow them to follow the experience.

  • Talk about the futur

    Inform public that another experience will be planned.
