Make my Way

Study case

Create a Web App for a startup

Brief

#safety #social #woman

We were asked to build a iOs prototype, using localization fonction and based on a community. This project is for a start-up. Wireframes will be made in Adobe Illustrator. The goal of this subject was to create fastly a concept salable, with a lot of restriction : small budget, no time for complex functionnalities, easy to use for the target user.

Process

1. Define the concept

  1. Subject choosen by the group : woman harassement

    We were four to collaborate for this project, half woman, half guys. Hajar and Cecile got the idea about an application helping them to go home safely without being disturbed or harassed. Carlos and I liked it (playing the bad guys to test the concept).

  2. Observation

    Increasement in the number of assaults against persons in 2014.

  3. Concept

    An application to help woman to go home safely that allows them to point on their map a risky area. To avoid bad guys using the application to stalk their victims, we decided that the user doesn't see others users. User can only see the different risky areas. We though about more complex functionnalities :

    • A swipe left to can be rescued by some others users
    • A swipe right to send a medical emergency signal
    • A button easy to click on the phone in your pocket who turned the phone's screen to red.
    • This same button could ask a police assistance, launch audio and camera, sending ID and localization of the phone owner in danger.

  4. For different reasons, the teacher put us back on the track of a start-up restrictions and on a simple concept/interface.

2. Where do we want to go

  • Inspiration benchmark

    To know if something was already tried, we benchmark the subject. Not much appeared for a safe way home of woman, but we found some similar applications like Waze to go home for drivers (avoid trafic, share itinerary, etc.) or Back me app to be link to a friend in case we feel not safe on the way.

    We checked the functionnalities, the interface to know what was good and what wasn't.

  • Target

    We didn't got time to make deep researches on our users, it wasn't the main point of the exercise. We took some classmates to interview. Our target was woman between 15 to 75+ years old, living in Paris area, connected and sociable.

  • Moodboard

    We created a shared Pinterest moodbard to know what we wanted to do on the graphical side taking in consideration our target users.

3. Build the solution

  • Persona

    We estalished "Lucy" our persona who could represent the target user. It's a young woman, studying and working who go out with some friends oflently. She's worried about the bad meeting which could happen when she go home in the evening.

  • Values

    We defined the values of our application to positionnate on the market.

  • Our added value

    Our application synchronizes the user with others users to have an update interactive map. Every user can point a warning on the map depending of the kind of danger. The warning area is ephemeral.

  • Tree

    Design a simple tree of the application to know how it's going to work.

  • Wireframes

    Design the different screens of the application, with the action's user possibilites.
