Study case

Build an e-business solution about the shopping list

#distribution #shopping list #optimization

Your agency works for a big retail brand. The brand has stores of various sizes ranging from hypermarkets to convenience store through the drive, and online business. It wants to provide a service to customers around the shopping list. The distribution brand had done preliminary work on the shopping list, but it is noted to be a failure and the idea was abandoned temporarily.

Your agency asks you to work "upstream" on the subject to provide a consistent user experience around the shopping list and avoid another failure. The objective is to propose provide users with great UX.

It is not asked to carry out a site or app in full but merely to sketch possible interactions.

Process

1. Define the scope of the project

  1. Define the context

    To bring a good solution, we need to understand the brand, its history, and its situation at the time we start working for. We need to understand client's and marketing objectives. We took in consideration technical restrictions.

  2. Benchmark the competing solutions and extract some trends

    To reach the client objectives, we take in consideration his full environnement to improve it in a unique way. We check and benchmark the competitors to understand the trends of the sector. We keep what is done right, throw away what is not.

  3. Check the innovation and evolution of the sector

    We also makes some research about innovation and tech improvement which could be interesting.

2. Research your user's expectations

  • Start guerilla interviews

    Start interview with users of our clients, feel their happiness and frustration, take note on interesting points who could bring our solution to the next level.

  • Drive users-test

    Leave tasks to users of the current product (and/or the new version), observe their reactions with the interface and state of mind. Save results.

  • Publish a survey on the subject

    Depending of the case (BtC), we might publish a survey about our subject to know a bit more from a different angle of our users.

  • Extract data and synthesize in statistics

    Conclude and reprezent with statistics all this researches target users.

  • Build a Persona (target user)

    Create a persona who represent an archetype of target users with his lifestyle. Sometimes, we make more than one depending of the kind of users using the product. It helps a lot for the design part.

3. Build the solution

  • Customer journey map

    Establish the full experience of the user's clients, with the good and bad times depending of the objectives.

  • Design wireframes taking in consideration the users researches

    Start designing wireframes to know visually how it's gonna work and if objectives are respected.

  • Test the solution until it fits the expectations

    Conduct new user's tests to see if the solution bring satisfaction. "Don't rest until it's elegant".

4. Present and explain the solution

  • Explain the result and what the client will win

    Present the UX process and the solution improved to our client. How we fix the problem.

  • Design a Brand Identity

    Brand Identity is the graphical layer who is going to give to the product an special ID and easy to recognize by users.
